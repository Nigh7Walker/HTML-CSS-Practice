Lazy Chicken Curry

This method of cooking chicken curry is the best method, especially coming home from a long days work. all you need is a oven and deep pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of chicken thighs
  • 3 stocks of celery
  • 3 Carrots
  • 1 Big onion
  • Some S&B curry Japanese curry mix
  • 4 cups of water
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons garlic powder

Directions

  1. prep oven to 400F
  2. Prepare your chicken thighs or your choice of meat
  3. mixed salt, pepper and garlic powder together in a cup
  4. Rub seasoning on chicken
  5. Cut veggies into small pieces
  6. place into a deep pan
  7. add 4 cups of water or until water just barely covers veggies
  8. Place in oven uncovered and cook for 40 mins
  9. after 40mins remove from oven and place on a burn medium high
  10. place 4 cubs of S&B Japanese Curry mix into the pan and wait till it disolves and stif constantly
  11. enjoy!!
