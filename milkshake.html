How to make a Milkshake

These Milkshake are thick, creamy, and so addictive that you'll be trying to drink every last drop.
They're the perfect treat for any time of the year, so here's How to Make a Milkshake. You only need two
ingredients and you can customize it however it you'd like with as many toppings as you'd like!

ingredients

  • 5 scoops ice cream about 1 3/4 cups
  • 1/4 cup milk

Directions

  1. Combine the ice cream and milk in a blender and blend on low until smooth
  2. Pour into a cup and garnish with whipped cream, sprinkles and a cherry if desired
