How to Cook Rice with a pot

This method of rice cooking is commonly used in most households who don't have a rice cooker.

Whats needed

  • Sauce Pan
  • Cup of rice
  • 1 1/2 cup of water

Diections

  1. Grab a medium size sauce pan one that can hold 2 litres
  2. pour in your 1 cup of rice
  3. Then pour in your water
  4. If you feel like its not enough water, trick to use is your finger knuckle place the tip of your finger on top of Rice and the first knuckle above your nail will be your guideline.
  5. Place Sauce pan on high heat, wait till boil then put on lid, turn heat on low for 10 mins after 10 mins turn off head wait another 10 minutes then serve
