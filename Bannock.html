Native Fry bread With Air fryer

This Fry bread is the staple in the First nations community across North America.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 cup of water or milk
  • Air Fryer
  • 1/4 of cooking oil

Directions

  1. Prepare Air Fry, set temps to 400F
  2. Put 2 cups of flour, salt and baking powder in a bowl
  3. Mix well, then pour in 1 cup of water or milk
  4. In a small cup pour in cooking oil
  5. Seperate dough into small pucks
  6. Brush oil onto dough pucks on each side
  7. Place dough pucks into Air Fryer Turning every 10 mins
  8. Once flipped brush oil top side
  9. Cook for another 10 mins or until golden brown
